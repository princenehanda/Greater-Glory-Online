Who We Are

We are a vibrant, family-oriented church dedicated to raising a generation of believers who live a life of divine glory. Our foundation is built on the Word of God, and our mission is to make a global impact by spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations.

Meet Our Pastors

Greater Glory Ministries is led by our founder, Pastor Isaac, a dynamic and anointed man of God with a heart for people. Together with his wife, they lead our church family with passion, integrity, and a deep commitment to the truth of God’s Word.

Our Statement of Faith

We believe in the Holy Trinity, the authority of the Holy Bible, the deity of Jesus Christ, salvation by grace through faith, and the sanctifying power of the Holy Spirit. We affirm the spiritual unity of all believers in Christ Jesus as one universal church.
