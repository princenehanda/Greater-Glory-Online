About Greater Glory Ministries

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a global community of believers passionately pursuing God's glory and manifesting His kingdom on earth. We are called to raise a generation of bold, Spirit-filled individuals who are empowered to transform their families, communities, and nations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip and empower believers through sound biblical teaching, dynamic worship, and authentic community. We are dedicated to creating an atmosphere where individuals can experience the tangible presence of God, discover their divine purpose, and be released to fulfill their unique calling in the world.

Our DNA

The core of Greater Glory Ministries is defined by our DNA: **Prayer, Worship, Word, and Community.**

Prayer

We believe in the power of fervent prayer to move mountains and change destinies.

Worship

Our worship is a passionate and heartfelt expression of love for God, inviting His presence into our midst.

Word

The Bible is our foundation and guide, providing wisdom and truth for every aspect of life.

Community

We are a family, committed to supporting and uplifting one another in our spiritual journeys.

What We Believe

  • We believe in **one God**, eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit.

  • We believe the **Bible** is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

  • We believe in the **deity of our Lord Jesus Christ**, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His atoning death, His bodily resurrection, and His ascension to the right hand of the Father.

  • We believe in the **sanctifying power of the Holy Spirit** by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a holy life.

  • We believe in the **spiritual unity of all believers** in Christ Jesus, forming one universal church.
