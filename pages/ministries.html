Our Ministries

We believe in empowering every member of our church to serve God and grow in their unique calling. Explore our ministries and find where you belong.

Daughters of Destiny

A vibrant community for women, empowering them to walk in their divine purpose and destiny.

Men of Valour

A fellowship of men dedicated to spiritual growth, leadership, and making a powerful impact in their families and communities.

Impact Generation

The youth and young adults' ministry, focused on raising passionate leaders who are sold out to God's vision.

Haven

A ministry dedicated to married couples, providing a haven of support, teaching, and fellowship to build strong, Christ-centered homes.
