What to Expect

Visiting a new church can be intimidating. Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you join us for a service.

Prayer Segment

Click to find out more

The Prayer Segment

Our service begins with a powerful time of corporate prayer, where we come together to lift up our needs, thank God for His goodness, and prepare our hearts to receive from Him. This is a time of unity and connection with God and one another.

Worship

Click to find out more

The Worship Segment

Led by our anointed choir and worship team, we enter into a heartfelt time of praise and worship. Our music is a mix of contemporary songs and powerful hymns, creating an atmosphere where you can freely express your adoration for God.

Testimony & Talkshow

Click to find out more

Testimony & Talkshow

We believe in celebrating God's goodness in our lives. This segment is dedicated to sharing inspiring testimonies and engaging in talkshow-style discussions that highlight God's work among us and encourage the body of Christ.

Teaching & Preaching

Click to find out more

The Teaching & Preaching Segment

Our pastors deliver dynamic, life-changing messages rooted in the Holy Bible. The teaching is practical, relevant, and designed to equip you to apply God’s Word to your everyday life and walk in the fullness of His glory.

Fellowship

Click to find out more

The Fellowship Segment

After the service, we invite you to stay and connect with us. This is a time to meet our pastors, chat with fellow members, and enjoy a time of community. We are a family, and we would love to get to know you!
